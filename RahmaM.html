The Maze Runner

üë§Author: James Dashner

üìöGenre: Dystopian, Science Fiction, and Mystery

The Maze Runner is a dystopian science fiction book written by James Dashner. The book follows a young boy named Thomas who finds himself in a mysterious ever-changing maze with no memory.He and a group of boys who call themselves Gladers are trapped inside a walled area with no clear way out. The Gladers have created their own society, and their main goal is to escape from the maze. Each day some of them venture into the maze to find an exit, however they must avoid the creatures in the maze called grievers. Thomas soon learns that his arrival might be the key to escaping, Thomas and the Gladers work together to survive and escape the maze.

This book is one of my favourite books as the book has engaging mystery which makes me want to keep on reading the book and find more about the maze.

The Fellowship of the Rings is one of the 3 volumes in the Lord of the rings book by J.R.R. Tolkien.
